# rakadatta.github.io

Personal academic website for **Raka Datta**, PhD Candidate in Economics at the Andrew Young School of Policy Studies, Georgia State University.

## About

Raka's research sits at the intersection of health economics and labor economics, with a focus on maternal health, reproductive policy, and labor market outcomes. She uses causal inference and applied microeconometric methods on large administrative and panel datasets to study how policy changes shape individual outcomes across generations.

**Research areas:** health economics, labor economics, maternal and infant health, reproductive policy, abortion policy, alcohol policy, intergenerational mobility, provider behavior.

## Site Contents

- **Bio** — overview of research focus and background
- **Working Papers** — six papers including work on abortion access and labor market outcomes, alcohol retail access and maternal/child health, physician response to RCT evidence, in-utero alcohol tax exposure, and neonatal mortality risk in home births and birth centers
- **Work in Progress** — ongoing projects on heat waves and fertility, the child penalty, paid leave, and post-Dobbs advertising
- **Research Interests** — health economics, labor economics, and public policy
- **Teaching** — instructor and TA roles at Georgia State University
- **Contact** — email, office address, CV, GitHub, Google Scholar, and X/Twitter
